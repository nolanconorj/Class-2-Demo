How to Make a Turkey Sandwich

  1. Get out two slices of rye bread
  2. Get out your turkey, American cheese, lettuce, tomato, and mayo
  3. Place three slicees of turkey onto one of the pieces of bread
  4. Place two slices of American cheese on top of the turkey
  5. Take some lettuce and two tomato slices and place them on top of the cheese
  6. Take the second piece of bread and smear some mayo on it with a knife
  7. Place the second slice on top of the first slice, cut sandwich in half, and VOILA!
